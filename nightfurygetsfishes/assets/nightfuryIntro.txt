The life in village of Berk is never peaceful. The Gaint Dragon has come back to revenge. While Hiccup and Toothless are going on an other adventure, he  sneaked into Berk, stole all fishes, and took away to the faraway forgotten land. Now Gaint Dragon is challenging Toothless again with the wisdom. 
Can Toothless defeat him and bring back the fishes ? Let the ride begins.
